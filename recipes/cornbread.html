Golden Sweet Cornbread

Description

This cornbread recipe is made from scratch with no mix. It's a real treat with chili, ribs, or barbecued chicken. You could even use this sweet, homemade cornbread to make stuffing for your Thanksgiving feast! My mom made this for me as a child, and now it's my family's favorite. Serve with butter, jam, honey, or your favorite spread.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup yellow cornmeal
  • ⅔ cup white sugar
  • 3 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • ⅓ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9-inch round cake pan.
  3. Whisk flour, cornmeal, sugar, baking powder, and salt together in a large bowl.
  4. Add milk, vegetable oil, and egg; whisk until well combined.
  5. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of the pan comes out clean, 20 to 25 minutes.
  7. Slice and enjoy!
